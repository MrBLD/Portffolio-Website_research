Curriculum Vitae
 

I earned my BE in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Tripura Institute of Technology, Agartala, India and M-Tech in Microelectronics and VLSI Design from National Institute of Technology, Agartala, India. I am currently in the verge of completing my doctoral studies on "High Speed Data Links" (Major: VLSI) under the supervision of Prof. (Dr) B.K. Bhattacharyya (Fellow IEEE) and Dr. P. Chakraborty at National Institute of Technology, Arunachal Pradesh, India, where I am also serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering since September 2013 and established the Integrated Circuit & System (i-CAS) Lab in 2015. Although my interests span almost all major aspects of VLSI Circuits and Systems, I am specifically interested in:

  • Digital Integrated Circuit
  • Current Mode Design
  • Clock Distribution and Gating Logic
  • Smart Electronic System Design
  
  • Low Power Techniques
  • Wireline Communication Circuits
  • Emerging Technology and Applications
I am always looking for dedicated and hard working students to join my group. If any solution on real life issues of circuit world catches your imagination and performing industrious research is your passion, I will be happy to get connected with you about the opportunities in my lab.
