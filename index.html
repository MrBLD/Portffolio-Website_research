Curriculum Vitae
 

Dr. Alak Majumder is a tenure track Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at National Institute of Technology (NIT), Arunachal Pradesh, India, where he directs the Integrated Circuit & System (i-CAS) Lab founded in 2015. He holds a Ph.D (Engg) in ECE from NIT Arunachal Pradesh, where he carried out research on "High Speed Data Links" under the supervision of Prof. (Dr) Bidyut K Bhattacharyya (Fellow IEEE) and Dr. P. Chakraborty. Earlier, he earned BE in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Tripura Institute of Technology, Agartala, India and M-Tech in Microelectronics and VLSI Design from National Institute of Technology, Agartala, India. He serves as a reviewer of many journals / transactions of IEEE, IET, Elsevier, Springer, World Scientific Publishers, Taylor & Francis, American Scientific Publishers, IoP and many others. He is also serving / served as an organizing member / reviewer of many international conferences of repute. Dr. Majumder received "Best Research/Paper Award" from IETE ASHTech-2018, Indore, ICCACCS-2018, Kolkata and ICEIC-2017, Phuket. He is a member of IEEE. Although his interests span almost all major aspects of VLSI Circuits and Systems, he is specifically interested in:

  • Analog & Digital Integrated Circuit
  • Current Mode Design
  • Clock Distribution and Gating Logic
  • Smart Electronic System Design
  
  • Low Power Techniques
  • Wireline Communication Circuits
  • Emerging Technology & Applications
He prefers dedicated and hard working students to join "The Team i-CAS". If any solution on real issues of Circuit World catches your imagination and performing extensive research is your passion, he will be happy to get connected with you about the possible Opportunities in our lab.
